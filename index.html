The Human Colossus Foundation

Synergy for the future

We entered a new age of civilization where technology and data brings us progress and value. Helps us to fly to the moon, explorer universe, understand nature surrounding us but as well help to cure cancer, keep people alive, educated them, give purpose and meaning in life - information is our most precious resource. To be able to use that information for good we need to steer the emerging ecosystem in the right direction towards prosperity, freedom, fair and educated society.

Our vision is to leverage emerging decentralized data economy around user centric services, data aggregation, self sovereign identity and distributed trust frameworks to improve existing infrastructure and build businesses on new models.

We need synergy within the ecosystem not only on the level of human beings but especially on the organizational level.

To put words into action, we established a non-profit foundation in Switzerland - as a relatively neutral ground with solid privacy regulations. The foundation is a home for all of the collaborative work which Colossus would do and through it we would share with and support different types of organization to build together a better world.

Decentralized Data Economy

Decentralized Data Economy (DDE) is a new way to look on the ecosystem which emerge from usage of data in connection with digital world. The Human Colossus Foundation's main goal is to faciliate creation of DDE and supporting comunities, businesses and individuals to being able to take part into new economy on equal and fair rights. We identify few main components which we would focus on to make that happen:

  • Overal Capture Architecture (OCA)
  • Trusted Digital Assistant (TDA)
  • Meaningful Service Providers (MSP)
  • Purpose based consent (PBC)
  • Purpose based Services (PBS)
